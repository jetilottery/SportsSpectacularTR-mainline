Sports Spectacular

Have a sporting good time as you collect balls to win prizes! Spin the wheel and watch as matching balls fill the prize meters, multipliers are revealed, and extra spins, instant wins, or extra balls are collected!

How to Bet

Ticket Cost

Displays the current ticket cost.

Press the left arrow (–) to decrease the ticket cost.

Press the right arrow (+) to increase the ticket cost.

Press BUY to deduct the ticket cost from the balance and play the game.

How to Play

Each game consists of 6 wheel spins. Press SPIN to spin the wheel. On each spin, 3 sections of the wheel spin: an Inner Wheel, Middle Wheel, and Outer Wheel. When the wheel stops, all 3 of the highlighted section of the wheel are evaluated according to the following:

Outer Wheel –3 Ball Symbols of varied types, or Trophy Bonus Symbols are highlighted on the wheel. Each highlighted ball or trophy fills 1 bar on the corresponding prize meter. Fill all bars on the prize meter to win the prize for that ball. Multiple prizes of different balls may be awarded. Each meter can only be won once. Fill all bars on the Trophy Bonus meter to trigger the Bonus game.

Middle Wheel – Awards a 1x, 2x, or 3x ball or trophy multiplier. The multiplier fills the corresponding prize meter for each highlighted ball or trophy on the Outer Wheel by 1, 2, or 3 bars respectively.

Inner Wheel – On each spin, 1 of 3 items are awarded:

• Free Spin Symbol – Adds +1 additional spin to the total remaining spins available

• Instant Win Symbol – Select 1 of up to 3 Symbols to reveal an instant prize

• Expand Symbol – Expands the highlighted section of the Outer Wheel, increasing the number of balls or trophies highlighted by 2 for a total of 5

Bonus Game

If all 5 bars on the Trophy Bonus prize meter are filled, the Bonus game plays immediately.

The Bonus game awards 3 initial spins on a 4x4 grid. Any Trophy Symbol that appears on the grid is highlighted and locks into that position on the grid for the remainder of the bonus, and the number of spins remaining is reset to 3 spins. If no Trophy Symbols appear on the grid, the number of spins remaining decreases by 1.

On the next spin, any additional Trophy Symbols that appear lock into position for the remainder of the bonus, and are added to the total, and the number of spins resets to 3.

Continue to spin until there are no spins remaining, or all 16 bars on the grid are filled with Trophy Symbols.

If 4 or more symbols are revealed, win the corresponding prize.

At the end of the bonus, the game returns to the base game to complete any spins remaining on the wheel. Only 1 Bonus game may be awarded.

The game ends after all awarded base games spins, and any triggered bonus game has completed. A message appears, indicating the game’s outcome.

Dismiss the win message and view the game’s outcome by pressing the message.

Auto Spin

Press AUTO SPIN to play the game to completion automatically. Game play continues until all selections have been made and any prizes have been awarded.

Rules

Misuse or malfunction voids all pays and plays.

Bonus wins are added to total winning prize values.

Instant wins are added to total winning prize values.

Player choices made during gameplay do not affect the game’s outcome.

Wins are shown in currency.

In this game, the wheel is a graphical animation for entertainment purposes only and does not represent an actual wheel. The chances of obtaining a specific segment are therefore not equiprobable.
